Description

Rhythm Doctor is the hardest one-button rhythm game out there. It's different from other rhythm games, in that all the music is composed specifically to match the level design, mechanics, and story. It is also a game where the player is expected to SLAM their spacebar, rather than just pressing it!

History

We won an IGF nomination for the game back in 2014, while we were still in school. We couldn't wait until we graduated so that we would be able to work full-time on it. Now that we've graduated, we're going full steam on development! Interview with Gamasutra: http://www.gamasutra.com/view/news/213064/Road_to_the_Student_IGF_Hafiz_Azman_on_Rhythm_Doctor.php

Features

  • 20+ handmade levels, each with their own story.
  • A large cast of doctors and patients feature in an epic overarching storyline.
  • Level editor included.
  • Play solo campaign or grab a friend for drop-in drop-out local multiplayer!
  • Blind mode, where the game can be played completely via audio cues only.
  • Challenge levels and New Game+ modes for the brave.
  • Full visual and audio calibration settings.
